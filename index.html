Aproaches To Learning

Approaches to Learning Springdale offers a 50% - 50% Bilingual Program where students learn in French and in English - This program, given to students in kindergarten, cycle 1, 2 and 3 of elementary, provides English instruction in: Mathematics, English Language Arts, Phys Ed, Music, Ethics and Religious Culture. French Instruction is in: Science, French Language Arts, Social Studies and Art.
